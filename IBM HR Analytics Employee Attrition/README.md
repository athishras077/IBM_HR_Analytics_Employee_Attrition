# IBM HR Analytics Employee Attrition & Performance Analysis

## 📌 Project Title

**End-to-End Data Analysis of Employee Attrition using IBM HR Analytics Dataset**

---

# 📖 Project Overview

Employee attrition is one of the most significant challenges faced by organizations. High employee turnover increases recruitment costs, reduces productivity, impacts team morale, and leads to the loss of experienced talent.

This project aims to perform an end-to-end data analysis on the IBM HR Analytics Employee Attrition & Performance dataset to identify the key factors influencing employee attrition. The insights generated from this analysis will help HR professionals and business leaders make informed decisions to improve employee retention.

The project follows the complete Data Analysis Lifecycle, including Business Understanding, Data Understanding, Data Cleaning, Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA), SQL Analysis, Dashboard Development in Power BI, and Business Recommendations.

---

# 🎯 Business Problem Statement

The Human Resources (HR) department is experiencing employee attrition, which affects organizational productivity and increases hiring and training costs.

The objective of this project is to analyze employee data to identify the major factors contributing to employee attrition and provide data-driven recommendations that can help improve employee retention and support strategic HR decision-making.

---

# 🎯 Business Objectives

The primary objectives of this analysis are:

* Calculate the overall employee attrition rate.
* Identify departments with the highest attrition.
* Analyze attrition across different job roles.
* Study the relationship between employee age and attrition.
* Examine the impact of monthly income on employee attrition.
* Analyze whether overtime contributes to higher attrition.
* Investigate the influence of business travel on employee turnover.
* Evaluate the relationship between work-life balance and attrition.
* Analyze the effect of years at the company on employee retention.
* Examine whether job satisfaction influences attrition.
* Study the relationship between performance ratings and employee attrition.
* Develop actionable business recommendations to reduce employee turnover.

# 📝 Assumptions

The analysis is based on the following assumptions:

* Each row in the dataset represents one employee.
* The dataset accurately reflects employee information.
* The "Attrition" column correctly indicates whether an employee has left the organization.
* Missing values, if present, are minimal and can be handled appropriately.
* The dataset represents historical employee records and is suitable for identifying attrition patterns.
* The analysis is descriptive and diagnostic rather than predictive.
